VIDEO TOUR OF GLA UNIVERSITY

GLA University is a private university in Uttar Pradesh, India that offers undergraduate courses in various disciplines. These include Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.), Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.), Bachelor of Arts (BA), and Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). The university also offers integrated dual-degree programs, Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), and Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) programs. The duration of most courses is three years, except for B.Tech.
