 ACADEMIC OFFICE

The academic office at GLA University is also responsible for ensuring that the academic programs offered by the university meet the standards set by regulatory bodies such as the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

The academic office at GLA University also provides support services to students, including academic advising, career counseling, and tutoring. The office works closely with faculty members to ensure that students receive the best possible academic experience and are prepared for success in their chosen careers.

Overall, the academic office at GLA University plays a critical role in ensuring the academic success and well-being of students at the university.
